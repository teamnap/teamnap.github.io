Nurse Assisting Platform

The NAP(Nurse Assisting Platform) Team is a team of portuguese young students devoted to improve the way nurses deal with shift changes.

With the use of technology to keep track of every patient's state and of the services provided to the patients, we intend to make nurses' lifes easier and patients happier.

Please contact us if you're interested in collaborating, funding or just giving some support/ideas :)
